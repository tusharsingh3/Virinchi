Meet the Founders
Vaibhav Kumar
Co-Founder | Architect | MURP | Commercial Design & Planning Specialist
Vaibhav is a commercially driven architect and an Urban and Regional Planning (MURP) postgraduate, bringing a unique blend of architectural creativity and urban foresight. He has worked extensively on large-scale commercial and office projects, contributing to both the design and construction stages of high-rise developments, business parks, and mixed-use layouts.

Vaibhav’s technical skills span AutoCAD, Revit, SketchUp, Lumion, and V-Ray, allowing him to bridge concept and construction with design precision and impactful visualizations. At Virinchi Architect, he leads commercial project planning, urban design integration, and visual communication—delivering market-responsive and functionally rich environments.
Sumit Chakrawarty
Co-Founder | Architect | PMC & BIM Strategist | Data Center Design Specialist
Sumit is a licensed architect and a postgraduate in Construction Engineering and Management, known for his strategic thinking and precision in project execution. With hands-on experience in the design and planning of data centers, he brings a deep understanding of technically complex environments where performance, security, and operational efficiency are critical.

His expertise lies in Project Management Consultancy (PMC), BIM-led workflows, feasibility studies, and cost optimization. He is highly proficient in Building Information Modeling (BIM), using it as a backbone for multidisciplinary coordination and digital project delivery. At Virinchi Architect, Sumit leads project strategy, execution planning, and innovation—particularly in data center infrastructure, office spaces, and residential developments.
Vertika Arya
Co-Founder | Interior Designer | M.Des. (Interior Design) | Experiential Space Curator
Vertika Arya holds a Master’s in Interior Design and brings artistic vision with technical depth to interior environments. Her design sensibility blends modern aesthetics with contextual relevance, transforming spaces into immersive and meaningful experiences.

With a strong foundation in residential, commercial, and workplace interiors, Vertika specializes in spatial planning, material exploration, lighting design, and user-centric customization. Her work focuses on harmonizing functionality with visual storytelling, making every project—from homes to offices—reflect both personality and purpose. At Virinchi Architect, she leads the Interior Design division, ensuring excellence from concept to execution.
About The Company

About Virinchi Architects

Virinchi Architects is a multidisciplinary architecture and design firm founded by three passionate and forward-thinking professionals—Sumit Chakrawarty, Vaibhav Kumar, and Vertika Arya. The firm was established with a shared vision: to create impactful, efficient, and timeless spaces that reflect both human aspirations and technical excellence.

At Virinchi Architects, we offer a holistic range of services across three core verticals:

  • Project Management Consultancy (PMC)
  • Architectural Planning & Design
  • Interior Design

With a deep belief in design as a transformative tool, we approach each project with critical thinking, contextual awareness, and a collaborative spirit. Our process integrates creativity with practicality, ensuring that each project—whether a technical data center or a cozy interior—is crafted with intention and delivered with precision.

Our Focus Areas

  • Data Centers:
    We understand the highly technical and secure nature of data center infrastructure. Our team is experienced in planning and coordinating data centers that meet international standards in redundancy, energy efficiency, and layout optimization.
  • Corporate and Commercial Spaces:
    From high-rise office towers to mixed-use commercial hubs, we deliver spaces that blend user comfort, branding identity, and operational efficiency—tailored to the business and its people.
  • Residential Architecture:
    We design homes that are functional, thoughtful, and emotionally resonant. From individual residences to multi-unit developments, our residential work reflects a strong understanding of user lifestyle and spatial quality.
  • Interior Design for All Typologies:
    Our interior design wing adds another dimension of detail and personalization. Whether it’s a home, an office, or a commercial space, we design interiors that reflect the client’s identity while enhancing usability and ambiance.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Multidisciplinary Collaboration:
    With our unique blend of backgrounds in architecture, urban planning, construction management, and interior design, we approach each project from multiple lenses—ensuring comprehensive solutions that address both macro and micro scales.
  • BIM-Driven Design:
    We are committed to technology-enabled design and delivery. Our use of Building Information Modeling (BIM) allows for smarter coordination, real-time visualization, and smoother execution of complex projects.
  • Client-Centric Approach:
    We believe that every project begins with listening. Our designs evolve through meaningful engagement with clients, stakeholders, and end-users, ensuring spaces that are responsive, sustainable, and emotionally engaging.
  • End-to-End Services:
    From the first sketch to the final handover, we stay involved at every stage—planning, design, coordination, documentation, visualization, project management, and handover—delivering full-cycle support for every project.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a new-generation design and project management firm that creates spaces of enduring value—spaces that are functional, beautiful, and responsive to the evolving needs of society, technology, and the environment.
